Vilaski motocycle track

The Motoclub has a Vilaski motocycle track in Valga County near Tsirguliina.

Features a soft and sandy 2.1km long and with enduro about 8.5km long. The start corridor is two to three hundred meters long. The track that has passed the bulldozer is pretty fast - By autumn it becomes very difficult and technical, then Aigar and Tanel Leok are here exercise doing. Track is sandy. Avo Leok often conducts youth training here.

When coming from Tartu along Valga-Tartu highway, if there is about 10km from Valga, before Emajõgi comes to turn Tsirguliina-Sangaste, 1-1.5 km and turn right, a few more kilometers and the trail is left under power lines.

Jaanikese motocycle Valley

Jaanikese Valley near Valga.

Valga Municipal Council has decided to buy back Jaanikese motocomplex, the best moto trail in the Baltic States, from a private owner. Ten years of unused trail is opened again.

Look more

The Jaanike track is well-traceable to the public and can be enjoyed by the pleasant curves and jumps.
